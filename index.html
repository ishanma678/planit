Features

A faster way to make plans actually happen.

Planit turns group planning into one clean flow: choose people, send the invite, and instantly see who’s coming.

👥

Invite everyone in one go

Add a friend’s name and number once, save them, and send the same invite to everyone at the same time.

🔔

Real-time responses

If your friend already has Planit, they get the invite in the app with a real notification and can respond right away.

⏰

Smart reminders

Keep the night moving with automatic reminders before the event starts.

📍

Place, time, message

Send the full plan in one clean invite with the location, time, and your message.

How it works

One smooth flow from idea to invite.

Designed for nights out, quick meetups, and spontaneous group plans that usually die in the chat.

1
Save your friends once
Because Planit is a PWA, it cannot access phone contacts directly. You add a friend’s name and number once, then it stays saved for next time.
2
Add the place, time, and quick message
Keep it simple and clear in seconds.
3
Send one invite to the whole group
If your friend has Planit, they receive the invite inside the app with a notification. If not, you can share it by text or another platform so they can install it and join.
4
Track accepted, pending, and expired invites
See who accepted, who is still pending, and which invites expired after the event time passes.
Install Planit

Open it in Safari. Add it to your home screen. Turn on notifications.

Planit works like an app, but it is installed from the browser. For the best experience, open it in Safari, tap Share, then tap Add to Home Screen. When you log in, use your email and phone number as your ID so friends can send you invites.

1

Open Planit in Safari on your iPhone

2

Tap the Share button

3

Choose Add to Home Screen

4

Log in with your email and phone number

5

Allow notifications for real invite alerts

Friends already on Planit get the invite in-app. Friends not in the system yet can receive a shared invite through text message or another platform, install Planit, and then get the invite too.
